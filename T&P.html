Training & Placement Cell

The Training & Placement Cell at New Satara College is committed to empowering students with the necessary skills and opportunities to launch their careers. We connect students with recruiters and help prepare them for the corporate world through professional development.

Objectives:

  • Enhance student employability through training and workshops
  • Build strong relationships with companies for recruitment
  • Organize campus placement drives and interviews
  • Assist students in internships and industrial visits

Services Provided:

  • Aptitude & Soft Skill Training
  • Mock Interviews & Group Discussions
  • Resume Writing Guidance
  • Technical Training by Industry Experts
