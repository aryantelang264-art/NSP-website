Departments

CO – Computer Engineering

Focuses on software development, system design, and computer networks. Prepares students for careers in programming, cybersecurity, AI, and data science.

IF – Information Technology

Covers networking, databases, web technologies, and IT services. Graduates excel in software support, cloud computing, and infrastructure management.

ME – Mechanical Engineering

Teaches mechanics, thermodynamics, and manufacturing systems. Ideal for careers in automotive, aerospace, energy, and design engineering.

CI – Civil Engineering

Focuses on infrastructure projects like buildings, roads, and bridges. Includes structural design, materials, surveying, and construction management.

ENTC – Electronics and Telecommunication

Combines electronics, communication systems, embedded tech, and digital signal processing. Prepares students for work in telecom, IoT, and circuit design.
