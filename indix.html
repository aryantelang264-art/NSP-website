Welcome to New Satara College

New Satara College of Engineering and Management (Polytechnic) was established in 2010.

To meet the needs of modern India, the institute provides quality technical education with highly qualified faculty.

It has a well-equipped modern library, effective teaching-learning process, and supportive facilities for student growth.

Located 9 km from Pandharpur in Korti village near the Mahadev temple, the college is surrounded by a lush green environment with hostels, buses, and a large playground.

The mission of the college is to advance knowledge and excellence in the field of higher education.
